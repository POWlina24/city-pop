How City Pop was revived by the internet

In 2018 Youtube algorithms launched City Pop songs into the wider collective consciousness, and almost immediately the songs surged in popularity. An especially known case was Maria Takehuchi’s song “Plastic Love” which showed up in everyone’s suggested videos at the time. It was so widely seen by YouTube users that it became a meme which only helped the song’s popularity grow.

Another recent well-known instance was on Tiktok in 2020 when people filmed their Japanese parents’ responses to Miki Matsubara’s song "Mayonaka no Door (Stay with Me)".
